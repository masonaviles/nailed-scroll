Meet the Villain

Hi my name is Shay, no the "Villain". That name is just for fun and aesthetics!

Deposit

A $15 deposit is due at the time of your booking. This will be applied to your final payment at the time of your appointment. This payment is non-refundable and transferable for one time, 48 hours prior to appointment. Any less will be a forfeit of deposit.

Remaining Balance

Remaining balance will be due in Cash, Apple Pay, or Zelle

Late Policy

If you are running late please let us know! We have a 10 minute grace period. After 10 minutes you will be charged a $10 late fee. After 20 mins you will be charged $20. 30 mins late the appointment will possibly be cancelled.

Cancellation

Cancelling appointment will result in a loss of deposit. No Call No Show = banned from booking. Deposit maybe transferred if client reschedules 48 hours before appointment
